Treating every customer the same wastes spend on the indifferent and under-invests in the valuable. Customer segmentation fixes that — and Apex Data Cloud makes sure it actually changes what you do, not just what you know.

## What we build

- **Behavioral & RFM segmentation** — group customers by what they actually do and how valuable they are.
- **Predictive segmentation** — [ML models](/services/machine-learning-consulting/) that group by likely future behavior: churn, purchase, upgrade.
- **Lookalike & high-value audiences** — find prospects who resemble your best customers.
- **Churn & retention segments** — identify at-risk customers early enough to act.
- **Activation** — push segments into your CRM, ad platforms, email, and CDP and tie them to campaigns.

## Our approach

We start from the decisions segmentation should drive, build segments on trustworthy data, and activate them where your team works. Then we measure impact with [marketing analytics](/services/marketing-analytics/) so segmentation is accountable to revenue.

## Outcomes

A clear, actionable segmentation model live in your stack, higher-performing targeting and retention, and a measurable lift in conversion and lifetime value.
