Agents are the next step beyond chat: AI that doesn't just answer but *acts*. Apex Data Cloud builds production AI agents that complete real workflows — with the orchestration, integration, and safety controls that separate a reliable system from a risky demo.

## What we build

- **Agent architecture & orchestration** — planning, memory, and control flow for single or multi-agent systems.
- **Tool & system integration** — connecting agents to your APIs, databases, and SaaS tools so they can do real work.
- **Human-in-the-loop** — approval gates and escalation for any action that carries risk.
- **Knowledge grounding** — [RAG](/services/rag-development/) so agents reason over your trusted information.
- **Guardrails & evaluation** — permissions, validation, audit logs, and task-completion metrics monitored in production.

## Our approach

We scope a single, bounded workflow with a measurable success criterion, build the agent with safety controls from day one, and prove task-completion rate against the current manual process before expanding scope. This keeps risk low and ROI visible.

## Outcomes

An agent that reliably completes a defined workflow, integrated with your systems, with the guardrails and monitoring to run it in production — plus a foundation to extend into adjacent workflows. Agents build on our [generative AI](/services/generative-ai-consulting/) and [data engineering](/services/data-engineering/) work.
