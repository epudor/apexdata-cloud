Governance has a reputation problem: too often it means slow committees and shelfware policies. Done well, it does the opposite — it gives teams trusted data faster and makes AI safe to deploy. That's the governance Apex Data Cloud implements.

## What we implement

- **Data quality frameworks** — measurable rules, monitoring, and ownership so quality is managed, not assumed.
- **Lineage & cataloging** — a searchable catalog and end-to-end lineage so people find data and trust where it came from.
- **Master data management** — one consistent definition of customer, product, and account across systems.
- **Consent & privacy** — GDPR/CCPA-aligned consent capture, data-subject request handling, and retention controls.
- **AI & model governance** — model documentation, bias checks, and human-in-the-loop controls for [AI](/services/ai-consulting/) deployments.

## Our approach

We start from your real risks and use cases, implement the minimum effective controls, and embed them into the [data pipelines](/services/data-engineering/) and [cloud platform](/services/cloud-architecture/) so governance is automated rather than manual.

## Outcomes

Trusted, well-documented data; demonstrable GDPR/CCPA compliance; cleaner master data; and the controls that make AI deployment defensible. Governance becomes an accelerator instead of a tax.
