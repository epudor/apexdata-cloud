Most AI initiatives stall not because the technology fails, but because the organization wasn't ready — unclear strategy, untrustworthy data, or no plan to operate what gets built. This assessment tells you where you stand and what to fix first.

## What it evaluates

The AI Readiness Assessment scores five dimensions:

- **Strategy** — clear, prioritized use cases tied to business value
- **Data** — availability, quality, and accessibility of the data AI needs
- **Infrastructure** — the [cloud and data platform](/services/cloud-architecture/) to build and run AI
- **Talent** — the skills to build, deploy, and operate AI (in-house or partnered)
- **Governance** — the [controls](/services/data-governance/) to deploy AI safely and compliantly

## What you'll get

A prioritized action plan: your strongest and weakest dimensions, the gaps to close first, and the highest-ROI [AI use cases](/services/ai-consulting/) to start with — within one business day, no obligation.
