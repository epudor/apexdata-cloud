Cloud data platforms are powerful — and easy to overspend on. This assessment finds where you're paying for capacity you don't use, and where performance and security can improve.

## What it covers

- **Cost** — right-sizing, storage tiering, and waste across compute and storage
- **Performance** — query efficiency and workload design
- **Security** — access controls, encryption, and isolation
- **Scalability & reliability** — readiness for growth and resilience
- **Architecture** — whether your [platform design](/services/cloud-architecture/) fits your workloads

## What you'll get

A prioritized list of savings and improvements with estimated impact — within one business day, no obligation.
