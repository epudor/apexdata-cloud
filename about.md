Apex Data Cloud is an **Orlando-based AI, data analytics, and machine learning consulting firm**. We help companies turn data into measurable revenue — combining the rigor of data scientists with the commercial instincts of operators.

## What we believe

Data and AI only matter when they change a decision and move a number. So every engagement starts by agreeing on the financial KPI it must improve — pipeline, CAC, LTV, conversion, cost, or cycle time — and ends with a system in production, not a slide deck.

## What we do

We work across the full stack: [AI](/services/ai-consulting/) and [machine learning](/services/machine-learning-consulting/) strategy and deployment; [generative AI](/services/generative-ai-consulting/), [RAG](/services/rag-development/), and [agents](/services/ai-agent-development/); [data engineering](/services/data-engineering/), [governance](/services/data-governance/), and [cloud architecture](/services/cloud-architecture/); and [customer segmentation](/services/customer-segmentation/) and [marketing analytics](/services/marketing-analytics/).

## How we work

A disciplined four-phase model — **Audit & Diagnose → Strategy & Blueprint → Build & Deploy → Measure & Scale** — keeps every initiative connected to outcomes, and transfers capability to your team along the way.

## Who we serve

Mid-to-large enterprises and growth-stage companies across [healthcare](/industries/healthcare/), [financial services](/industries/financial-services/), [retail](/industries/retail/), [manufacturing](/industries/manufacturing/), [technology](/industries/technology/), and [real estate](/industries/real-estate/) — based in [Orlando](/locations/orlando/), serving [Central Florida](/locations/central-florida/), [Florida](/locations/florida/), and nationwide.
