**Generative AI** is a category of artificial intelligence that creates new content — text, images, code, audio, and more — by learning patterns from large datasets. Its best-known form is the [large language model](/glossary/large-language-model/).

## Why it matters

Generative AI can draft, summarize, translate, answer questions, write code, and assist analysis — scaling knowledge work in ways previous automation couldn't. The business challenge is making it *reliable*: grounding it in your data with [RAG](/glossary/retrieval-augmented-generation/), evaluating outputs, and controlling cost and risk.

## Generative vs. predictive AI

Predictive AI (traditional machine learning) forecasts or classifies — churn, demand, risk. Generative AI *creates*. Many real solutions combine both. See the difference in our [generative AI consulting](/services/generative-ai-consulting/).
