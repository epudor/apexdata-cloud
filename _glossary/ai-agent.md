**An AI agent** is a system that uses a [large language model](/glossary/large-language-model/) to reason about a goal and then take actions — calling tools, querying data, or invoking APIs — across multiple steps to complete a task, rather than just returning a single answer.

## Why it matters

A chatbot answers; an agent *does work*. Agents can look things up, fill in records, trigger workflows, and decide the next step — automating tasks that combine rules with judgment. They're evaluated on task completion, not conversation quality.

## What makes them safe

Production agents need guardrails: scoped permissions, human approval for high-stakes actions, input/output validation, audit logging, and continuous evaluation. Designing these in is central to [AI agent development](/services/ai-agent-development/).
