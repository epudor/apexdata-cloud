Technology and SaaS companies are data-native — but turning product and usage data into growth, and shipping AI features that actually work, is its own discipline. Apex Data Cloud helps on both fronts.

## Where AI and data create value

- **Product-led growth analytics** — find the activation and usage signals that predict conversion and retention.
- **Churn & expansion modeling** — predict at-risk and expansion-ready accounts and act in time.
- **Trial-to-paid & PQL scoring** — focus sales and onboarding where they'll convert.
- **AI product features** — [generative AI](/services/generative-ai-consulting/), [RAG](/services/rag-development/), and [agents](/services/ai-agent-development/) embedded in your product.
- **Revenue & usage forecasting** — sharpen planning and investor reporting.

## Our approach

We connect product, billing, and CRM data ([data engineering](/services/data-engineering/)), model the growth levers ([ML](/services/machine-learning-consulting/)), and — when relevant — help you ship AI features with proper evaluation and unit economics.
