Real estate and PropTech run on leads, timing, and local market knowledge. AI sharpens all three. Apex Data Cloud helps brokerages, platforms, and PropTech firms convert more of the right opportunities at lower cost.

## Where AI and data create value

- **Buyer & seller intent modeling** — predict who's likely to transact and when.
- **Lead scoring & routing** — focus agent time on the highest-probability leads.
- **Market intelligence** — analytics on pricing, inventory, and demand by market.
- **Marketing attribution** — see which [campaigns](/services/marketing-analytics/) generate transactions, not just clicks.
- **Customer & agent assistants** — [RAG-powered](/services/rag-development/) answers grounded in listings and policy.
