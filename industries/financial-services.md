Financial services has the data, the budget, and the regulatory scrutiny that make disciplined AI essential. Apex Data Cloud helps banks, insurers, and fintechs capture value while keeping models explainable, governed, and compliant.

## Where AI and data create value

- **Fraud & risk** — fraud detection, credit and default risk, and AML monitoring.
- **Acquisition & retention** — propensity, churn, and next-best-action models that grow value per customer.
- **Compliant personalization** — relevant offers and experiences within consent and fair-treatment constraints.
- **Forecasting** — revenue, demand, and capacity forecasts that sharpen planning.
- **Advisor & support copilots** — [RAG assistants](/services/rag-development/) grounded in approved policy and product content.

## Governance and explainability first

Regulated decisions need to be explained and defended. We pair [machine learning](/services/machine-learning-consulting/) with [data governance](/services/data-governance/), interpretable methods, and thorough model documentation so deployments hold up to scrutiny.
